Hiring

WildOpen Helps You Find The Talent You Need With A Sourcing Solution That Meets Your Needs

Work with our recruiters who have relationships with the open source community and AI-driven solutions to help you find the experts you need to hire.

Talk with us about Hiring
Hiring Solutions
Project Work

You have identified the specific skills and experience you need to fill a project in your organization. Next, work with WildOpen to identify the team of professionals that will help you fulfill and support your project.

Direct Hire

You have identified the specific skills and experience you need to fill a position in your organization. Partner with WildOpen to find a qualified candidate with the right skills and expertise.

Contract-to-Hire

We assess your needs and find a candidate who best fits the role. Then, we’ll assign them to your company on a contract basis. If you are happy with the candidate you can hire them permanently.

Residency

We assess your needs and find a candidate who may need some training and extra management but otherwise fits the role. We assign them to your company along with a mentor who helps them deliver for you. If you are happy with the candidate you can make them a full-time offer.

We Help You Find The Right Talent

Hiring technical experts is often difficult. Hiring open source software experts can be doubly so. With commercial software, there are often training courses and certification programs that tell you a candidate’s level of expertise. Or you can look at their work history for proof. With open source software, there may be few courses and nearly no certifications. Further, an expert’s experience may have been gained through volunteer contributions to the open source library with little documentation.

WildOpen has the experience with and close connections to the open source software communities to understand a candidate’s level of expertise. We can find the candidates you are looking for and help assess their qualifications.

Talent Solution Benefits

Expedite Interview Process

WildOpen thoroughly interviews each candidate to ensure they are well qualified for the job before your interview.

Budget Flexibility

Give yourself time to work out exactly what is required in a full-time resource by hiring a contractor first or starting a project with WildOpen.

Staffing Flexibility

WildOpen allow you to quickly increase or decrease resources when needed based on business demand – as a talent cloud.

Savings on Specialized Skills

WildOpen allows you to ramp up resources you may only need for a short-term project.

Accommodate Growth

A partnership with WildOpen gives you the flexibility to start new projects and grow your team on short notice.

Guaranteed Placement 

WildOpen guarantees the quality of contractors and resident experts and will substitute them with other contractors if required.

Try Before Hiring

WildOpen is flexible in allowing you to contract with a candidate for a set period before hiring them.
